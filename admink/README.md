## About The Project

We want to **help administrators** of tattoo studios to **grow faster** and **multiply profits**!

Manage clients, artists, budgets, scheduling and measure the development of your business with data.

_Project Link: [Admink on GitHub](https://github.com/tteixeira47/admink)_
### Built With

* [PHP](https://www.php.net/)
* [Laravel](https://laravel.com)
* [Bootstrap](https://getbootstrap.com)
* [MariaDB](https://mariadb.com/)
